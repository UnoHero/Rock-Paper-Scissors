Rules


Objective:
The objective of the game is to win the best of 3 rounds against the computer opponent.

Moves:
The player can choose one of three moves: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The computer opponent will also choose one of the three moves.

Rules for Determining Round Winner:
Rock beats Scissors. Scissors beats Paper. Paper beats Rock.

Gameplay:
The player and the computer both select their moves simultaneously. The winner of each round is determined based on the rules mentioned above. If the player's move defeats the computer's move, the player wins the round. If the computer's move defeats the player's move, the computer wins the round. If both the player and the computer choose the same move, the round is a tie and no one wins.

Winning the Game:
The game is played in a best-of-3 rounds format. The first player (either the player or the computer) to win 2 rounds wins the game. If the player wins 2 rounds, they win the game. If the computer wins 2 rounds, the computer wins the game.

User Interface:
A timer wil start to count down from 3. On 1. Displays the options (Rock, Paper, Scissors) to the player. After the player's choice is made, reveal the computer's choice. Displays the result of the round (player wins, computer wins, or tie). A tally score wil be shown. Displays the final result of the game after 3 rounds.

Example Gameplay:
Round 1: Player selects Rock, Computer selects Scissors. Player wins. Round 2: Player selects Paper, Computer selects Paper. It's a tie. Round 3: Player selects Scissors, Computer selects Rock. Computer wins.
